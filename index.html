About Us

LYNQ is an entertainment platform based in Dubai, focused on providing free invitations for ladies to restaurants, lounges, clubs, and beach clubs. The platform aims to help venues advertise themselves by allowing guests to share their experiences on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok. LYNQ operates as a social platform where people can sign up, become members, and enjoy the services offered by the platform and its partner venues. To become a member, individuals need to sign up and have their membership approved after their profiles are reviewed. The main goal of LYNQ is to ensure guest satisfaction and provide a platform for venues to effectively promote their businesses.
